Designed for discerning individuals

Is there a typical traveler who chooses the carbon luggage from Swiss Luggage SL? Yes, they are frequent travelers with high standards for comfort, quality and aesthetics, and good design. The cases from Swiss Luggage SL are easier to pack, close and open. Neither cumbersome to carry nor awkward to roll, they are ergonomically perfect. They are light and thus save weight. They are extremely tough and, moreover, Swiss Luggage SL cases sport a timeless design that is beyond comparison.

The independent traveler

AILEEN ZUMSTEIN, 40, COMMUNICATION ADVISER

The communication strategist spends a lot of time in airplanes, trains and on public transport. “Luggage is actually an impediment as the means of transportation are becoming more full all the time,” she says, and adds: “The trolleys and cases from SL are a considerable relief; they are lightweight and fantastically easy to handle. My Trolley 55 is a panacea: commercial documents, fact sheets and files, clothes – everything fits inside and is protected against theft and any imaginable event. And yes, the design is state-of-the-art.”

The creative traveler

IVETT PERLAKY, 27, GRAPHIC ARTIST, ILLUSTRATOR AND INTERIOR DESIGNER

Ivett crosses borders on a weekly basis. Hungary, Switzerland, Germany – this creative and linguistic all-round talent is continuously on the move. “I discovered Swiss Luggage SL during a business trip to a colleague in Zurich, and realized immediately: I have to have that!” Swiss Luggage SL has been her loyal companion since 2016, and she has never been let down: “Of course, the design appealed to me in the first place, but now it’s their practical advantages – lightweight, robust, easy to handle – that keep me holding tight to my SLs!”
