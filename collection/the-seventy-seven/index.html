Security plus

ANTI-THEFT SAFETY WIRE

An ingenious steel wire that is around 80 cm long and comes with its own code ensures an even higher level of security. In the waiting lounge or at the airport gate, for example. This special wire is discreetly sunk into the center of the handle and can be pulled out when needed. Once attached to any immovable object, this anti-theft device ensures maximum security for your luggage while you browse the duty free shop or stroll to the coffee bar. MATERIALS

Without using the very best materials, it would not be possible to build the outstanding and most durable luggage system in the world. Every material is selected on grounds of quality. Carbon fiber is light, impact-proof and earthquake-resistant. It is protected by a (rolled!) cast lamination. This makes splintering impossible and ensures that the case is scratch-resistant, polishable and self-repairing (it is also used in high-tech sporting equipment such as skis).

This is due to the perfect center of gravity, which is achieved through the asymmetrical cutting of the case shells and the layout of the components. Handles, locks and rollers are thus used to ensure an optimal center of gravity.
