THE COMPANY

Swiss Luggage SL
A child of the new millennium

You will know that we Swiss leave nothing to chance. We take the time to allow an idea and a product to mature to perfection.
Swiss Luggage SL first emerged as a product idea and was then developed as a limited company in the environment of the Technology Park in the Swiss capital of Bern. This knowledge hub, which was founded by research and industry, combines the know-how of innovative engineers from every modern high-tech discipline, and enjoys close ties to the University of Bern, engineering schools, and of course all other leading Swiss institutions in research and education.
A Bern-based company involved in both motor racing and the distribution of high-quality luggage first came up with the idea of exploring carbon for use in suitcases. This idea sparked a creative fire and eventually gave rise to the Swiss Luggage SL limited company at the turn of the millennium.

THE PATH TO SUPERLATIVES

Many years of testing, innovation and patents

Uncompromisingly secure, exclusive and high-quality – this is what distinguishes Swiss Luggage SL from ordinary mass-produced goods in the upper price segment. Two aspects are decisive for quality: Swiss Luggage SL is sturdier and more robust than other products. And should something happen nonetheless, the repair work is carried out in a quick, uncomplicated and competent manner. Naturally, even beyond the comprehensive 5-year warranty.

PHILOSOPHY AND VISION

Every single suitcase must also have a soul

If you purchase a suitcase from Swiss Luggage SL, you will probably start a lifelong love affair. At the very first touch you can feel that this piece of luggage has personality, an unmistakable and distinctive profile, an athletic-looking silhouette, and unique characteristics. Every detail discreetly radiates the intrinsic values of Swiss Luggage SL and awakens passion: quality and security at their most exquisite.

And while this luggage system is the best in its class, it’s by no means the most expensive – entirely in keeping with the vision of Swiss Luggage SL. Swiss Luggage SL proves that top quality does not have to be a luxury. Buying a case from this luggage system is first and foremost an act of prudence. If cost/benefit considerations are important to you, a case from Swiss Luggage SL is your best bet.
